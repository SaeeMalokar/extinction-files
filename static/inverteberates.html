Overview of Extinct Invertebrates

Invertebrates, a diverse group of animals without backbones, have been affected by climate change, habitat loss, and pollution. Their extinction has significant ecological consequences as they play vital roles in ecosystems, such as pollination, nutrient cycling, and serving as food for other species.
