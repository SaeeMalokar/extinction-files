Overview of Extinct Marsupials

Marsupials, including kangaroos, koalas, and wombats, are a diverse group of mammals primarily found in Australia and nearby islands. Due to habitat destruction, climate change, and human interference, many marsupial species have gone extinct. Their extinction has significant ecological consequences.
