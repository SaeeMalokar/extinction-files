Fun Facts

  • The woolly mammoth was around 13 feet tall!
  • Some species of dodo birds were flightless.
  • The passenger pigeon was once the most common bird in North America.
  • Neanderthals lived in Europe until about 40,000 years ago.
  • The Tasmanian tiger, or thylacine, was declared extinct in the 20th century.
